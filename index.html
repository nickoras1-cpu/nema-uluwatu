The Details

Everything you need to know about staying at Uluwatu's premier boutique accommodation.

Are you close to the beach, restaurants, and bars?

Yes, we are right in the heart of it. Nema. Uluwatu is a short 4-minute walk to Thomas Beach. You won't need to travel far for world-class dining or nightlife—we are just steps away from Uluwatu's most sought-after cafes, restaurants, and clifftop beach clubs.

Is Nema. Uluwatu suitable for solo female travelers?

Absolutely. We prioritize your peace of mind so you can fully relax. Nema. Uluwatu is a highly private sanctuary featuring dedicated overnight security. Our location also makes independent exploration effortless—you are just a short, safe stroll away from Thomas Beach and Uluwatu’s vibrant cafe and dining scene, meaning you never have to travel far.

Is the Wi-Fi fast enough for remote work?

Absolutely. We know how crucial connectivity is, so we've equipped the entire property with highly reliable 50mbps fiber-optic Wi-Fi. Combined with the sunlit work nooks in our studios, it’s the ideal setup for digital creatives.

What kind of vibe can I expect?

We provide the high-end amenities of a boutique hotel with the relaxed, unpretentious freedom of a premium homestay. It’s a stylish, aesthetic environment perfect for relaxing by our 16m pool during the day before heading out to experience Uluwatu's vibrant social scene at night.

Can I rent the entire property for a retreat?

Yes. You can rent all 12 studios for a complete property buyout. With our expansive pool, daily housekeeping, and private grounds, Nema. Uluwatu serves as the ultimate luxurious sanctuary for hosting group wellness escapes or private retreats.
